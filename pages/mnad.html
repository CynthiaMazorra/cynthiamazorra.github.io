MNAD: The Evolution of Objects is a project that digitally orders what is not physically possible. Under the limitations of space redistribution due to the consideration of the National Museum of Decorative Arts as National Heritage, we rethought its structure with a digital paradigm of collectibles with a connection to the physical.

Through interactive “bridges” between the current exhibition and the proposed digital approach, we give a new value to the museum, extending the information of the objects on display with 3D visualizations and prolonging their presence beyond the walls of the MNAD, with both physical and digital collectibles, which can be viewed through a dedicated app.
