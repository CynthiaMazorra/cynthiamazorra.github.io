Artificial Intelligence and User

Experience Design: Study and

Prototype of the Sneak App

Sneak:

Methodology. Strategy. Scope and Structure. Visual identity. Prototype. See more

This Final Degree Project investigates the impact of artificial intelligence on UX/UI design . Throughout the project, the key concepts of AI applied to design are examined and various platforms and tools that designers can currently use to optimize their creative processes are explored.

The objective of this thesis was to analyze how companies are using AI today, addressing both its ethical implications and the opportunities for UX/UI designers to take advantage of this technology. Down below, the most relevant parts of the development of the prototype (Sneak) assisted by AI will be shown following the methodology of Jesse James Garrett, thus allowing the design process to be structured in a clear and effective way.

Sneak is a mobile application designed to share secrets anonymously within a community. Users can post and read confessions or stories without revealing their identity, creating a safe and discreet space for personal expression. The app offers moderation tools to ensure a respectful environment and the option to interact through reactions or comments, encouraging anonymity and the freedom to share without judgment.
